Piccola
Piccola is our Micromouse. It means small. With piccola we learnt a lot. A lot about PCB designs, hardware designing and specially we learnt about our new favourite micro-controller, STM32 blue pill. Building piccola was so interesting. We spent almost 3 months working day and night. It took us one month to design the hardware and the PCB. After we got over PCB fabricated, the real game begun. We were able to build hardware of piccola within a week. Then it was all about coding and it was even more interesting. When building piccola, lot of time was invested in tunning. We wanted to make all the moves very precise and predictable. Throughout 3 month the experience we got is incredible.
Online shoppers intention
Predicting online shoppers purchase intention just by looking at the data is almost imposible. But with a machine learning approach, it's not that hard. However it take some effort. Infact when we tried doing it, we took three steps. Data pre-processing, feature engineering and prediction model building. Following these three steps we were able to predict with a accuracy of 95%.

To be honest, this is one of the most interesting projects we have done. It would be great if you check out our work. Read our articles we wrote on this. It's fun!
